The Artisan’s Pour: Hario V60

This is a "Special" type brew with a "Hard" difficulty. It requires precision and technique to unlock the bright, floral notes of high-quality beans.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 5 minutes
  • Preparation: 2 minutes
  • Brewing: 3 minutes

Ingredients

  • 15g Medium-Fine coffee (table salt texture)
  • 250ml Filtered water (94°C)
  • V60 Paper Filter & Gooseneck kettle

Instruction

  1. Prep: Rinse the paper filter with hot water to remove any paper taste and warm the dripper.
  2. The Bloom: Add coffee. Pour 30g of water and wait 45 seconds to let the gases escape.
  3. The Spiral: Slowly pour the remaining water in steady, outward circles. Don't touch the edges.
  4. The Draw: Let the water finish dripping. The coffee bed should be flat and clean at the bottom.

Pros & Cons

Clarity of Flavor Extreme
Technique Level Master
Effort Required High
