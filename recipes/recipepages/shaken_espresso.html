Brown Sugar Shaken Special

This is a "Special" type brew with a "Medium" difficulty. It mimics high-end cafe drinks using simple tools like a mason jar.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 5 minutes
  • Preparation: 3 minutes
  • Shaking: 2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 Shots strong espresso (or Moka Pot)
  • 1 tbsp Brown sugar & Cinnamon
  • Oat milk & Large ice cubes

Instruction

  1. Sweeten: Brew hot coffee directly over the brown sugar so it dissolves instantly.
  2. The Shake: Add the mix to a jar with ice. Shake hard for 30 seconds until a thick foam forms.
  3. The Pour: Strain into a glass with fresh ice. Top with cold milk to see the marbling.

Pros & Cons

Sweetness High
Cafe Look Perfect
Complexity Medium
