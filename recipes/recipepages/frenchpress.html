The Daily Plunge: Classic French Press

The French Press is a "Normal" type brew with an "Easy" difficulty. It uses full immersion to extract deep, chocolatey notes and a heavy mouthfeel.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 8 minutes
  • Preparation: 2 minutes
  • Brewing: 6 minutes
ADVERTISEMENT

Ingredients

  • 30g Coarsely ground coffee
  • 500ml Filtered water (92Â°C)
  • French Press & Stirring spoon

Instruction

  1. Preheat: Rinse the press with hot water to warm the glass, then discard the water.
  2. Add Coffee: Put the 30g of coarse grounds in the bottom of the vessel.
  3. The Pour: Add all 500ml of water quickly to ensure all grounds are fully saturated.
  4. The Wait: Place the lid on (do not plunge) and let it steep for 4 minutes.
  5. The Plunge: Gently stir the top "crust," wait 1 minute for grounds to settle, then plunge slowly.
