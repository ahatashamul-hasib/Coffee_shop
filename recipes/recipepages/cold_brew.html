Overnight Cold Brew Concentrate

This is a "Normal" type brew with an "Easy" difficulty. It’s perfect for those who want a smooth, low-acid coffee ready in the fridge.

Preparation time

  • Total: 12 to 24 Hours
  • Active Prep: 5 minutes
  • Steeping: Overnight

Ingredients

  • 100g Very coarse coffee (chunky)
  • 800ml Cold filtered water
  • Large glass jar & Strainer

Instruction

  1. The Mix: Add coffee and water to the jar. Stir gently to ensure all grounds are wet.
  2. The Steep: Seal the jar and leave it in the fridge for at least 12 hours.
  3. The Filter: Pour the mixture through a fine mesh or cloth. This is a concentrate—add milk to serve.

Pros & Cons

Acid Levels Lowest
Convenience High
Prep Effort Low
